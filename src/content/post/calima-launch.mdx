We're very excited to announce new collaborations to support our community

AWSM is a new startup incubator and fund to support BIPOC founders

AWSM Weekend is a 3 day event where participants work together with mentors to launch new startups

### AWSM FCTRY

AWSM Factory is opening up physical locations to accelerate the go to market of startups in our community

## [Santo Cabrón](https://awsm.vc)

Santo Cabrón is an artists community with designers, illustrators, filmmakers, producers and architects
