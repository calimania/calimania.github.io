## A Community of Innovators

The Latinx Tech Group is more than just a WhatsApp chat—it's a vibrant network of founders, investors, and operators who are passionate about technology and innovation.

This community is designed to be a supportive space where members can connect, share knowledge, and collaborate on exciting projects.

## Multiple Groups for Specific Interests

To ensure meaningful interactions and targeted support, the Latinx Tech Group is divided into multiple sub-groups. These include:

- **Local Groups**: Connect with tech enthusiasts and professionals in your area. Share local events, meetups, and opportunities to collaborate on projects.
- **Founders Group**: A dedicated space for startup founders to share experiences, seek advice, and find potential co-founders or team members.
- **Investors Group**: Connect with investors who are looking to support innovative projects and discover new investment opportunities within the Latinx tech community.
- **Operators Group**: A space for operators to discuss best practices, share operational insights, and support each other in driving business success.
